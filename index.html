TRAVEL THROUGH THE SPACE

☀️ Sun

  • Nickname: “The Heart of the Solar System”
  • Type: Star (G-type main-sequence)
  • Age: ~4.6 billion years
  • Diameter: ~1.39 million km
  • Distance from Earth: ~150 million km
  • Fact: Contains 99.8% of the Solar System’s mass and powers life on Earth.

🌕 Moon

  • Nickname: “Luna”
  • Type: Natural satellite of Earth
  • Age: ~4.5 billion years
  • Diameter: 3,474 km
  • Distance from Earth: ~384,400 km
  • Fact: Influences Earth’s tides and is the only celestial body humans have visited.

🪐 Mercury

  • Nickname: “Swift Planet” (shortest year: 88 days)
  • Type: Terrestrial planet
  • Age: ~4.5 billion years
  • Diameter: 4,880 km
  • Distance from Sun: ~58 million km
  • Fact: Smallest planet, with extreme temperature differences.

🌑 Venus

  • Nickname: “Earth’s Twin” (similar size, but hostile)
  • Type: Terrestrial planet
  • Age: ~4.5 billion years
  • Diameter: 12,104 km
  • Distance from Sun: ~108 million km
  • Fact: Hottest planet (~465 °C), thick CO₂ atmosphere, surface pressure ~92× Earth’s.

🌍 Earth

  • Nickname: “Blue Planet”
  • Type: Terrestrial planet
  • Age: ~4.54 billion years
  • Diameter: 12,742 km
  • Distance from Sun: ~150 million km (1 AU)
  • Fact: Only known planet with liquid surface water and life. Atmosphere: 78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen.

🔴 Mars

  • Nickname: “The Red Planet”
  • Type: Terrestrial planet
  • Age: ~4.6 billion years
  • Diameter: 6,779 km
  • Distance from Sun: ~228 million km
  • Fact: Known as the “Red Planet,” has the tallest volcano (Olympus Mons) and largest canyon (Valles Marineris) in the Solar System. Two small moons: Phobos and Deimos.

🌐 Jupiter

  • Nickname: “The Giant”
  • Type: Gas giant
  • Age: ~4.6 billion years
  • Diameter: 139,820 km (largest planet)
  • Distance from Sun: ~778 million km
  • Fact: Largest planet, mass ~318 Earths, has >90 moons (incl. Ganymede – largest moon in Solar System). Famous for the Great Red Spot storm.

💍 Saturn

  • Nickname: “The Ringed Planet”
  • Type: Gas giant
  • Age: ~4.5 billion years
  • Diameter: 116,460 km
  • Distance from Sun: ~1.43 billion km
  • Fact: Iconic ring system made of ice and rock, has >80 moons (Titan is bigger than Mercury). Density is so low it would float in water.

🌀 Uranus

  • Nickname: “The Sideways Planet”
  • Type: Ice giant
  • Age: ~4.5 billion years
  • Diameter: 50,724 km
  • Distance from Sun: ~2.87 billion km
  • Fact: Axis tilt of 98° → rotates on its side. Pale blue color due to methane. Has faint rings.

🌊 Neptune

  • Nickname: “The Windy Planet”
  • Type: Ice giant
  • Age: ~4.5 billion years
  • Diameter: 49,244 km
  • Distance from Sun: ~4.5 billion km
  • Fact: Strongest winds in Solar System (up to 2,100 km/h), deep blue color, discovered in 1846 by mathematical prediction.

❄️ Pluto (Dwarf Planet)

  • Nickname: “The Forgotten Planet” (or “King of the Kuiper Belt”)
  • Type: Dwarf planet (Kuiper Belt)
  • Age: ~4.5 billion years
  • Diameter: 2,377 km
  • Distance from Sun: ~5.9 billion km (elliptical orbit)
  • Fact: Reclassified as a dwarf planet in 2006. Has five known moons (largest: Charon). Sometimes closer to the Sun than Neptune due to its orbit.
